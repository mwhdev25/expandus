## 🎯 Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Writing & Content Creation">
    Tools like **ChatGPT** and **Claude** excel at drafting blog posts, marketing copy, emails, and creative writing. Claude's long-context window makes it ideal for editing long documents.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Research & Summarization">
    **Perplexity AI** is purpose-built for research — providing cited, real-time answers. Claude and ChatGPT also handle document summarization extremely well.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Coding & Development">
    ChatGPT's code interpreter, paired with **GitHub Copilot** (see Engineering category), makes LLMs indispensable for developers at every skill level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data Analysis">
    ChatGPT's Advanced Data Analysis mode can interpret CSV files, generate charts, and produce statistical summaries without writing any code.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
