## 🎯 Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App-to-App Integration">
    **Zapier** and **Make** are the top choices for connecting SaaS apps. Zapier is easier; Make is more powerful for complex multi-step workflows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Developer Automation">
    **n8n** and **Pipedream** give developers the flexibility to write code inside automation workflows, blending no-code speed with full programmability.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sales & Outreach Automation">
    **Bardeen** shines for sales teams — automating LinkedIn scraping, CRM updates, and outreach workflows directly from the browser.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Process Automation">
    **Relay.app** is purpose-built for workflows that need human checkpoints — like approval flows, onboarding processes, and cross-team handoffs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
