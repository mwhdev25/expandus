## 🎯 Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Assisted Coding">
    **GitHub Copilot** and **Cursor** are the top two picks for AI-assisted development. Copilot works inside your existing editor; Cursor is a full IDE rebuild around AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deployment & Hosting">
    **Vercel** is the standard for deploying Next.js and React apps. Supabase handles your backend — database, auth, and APIs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Project & Issue Tracking">
    **Linear** has become the preferred tool for software-first companies — with its speed, keyboard shortcuts, and clean UX setting a new standard.

  <Accordion title="Terminal & CLI Tooling">
