## 🎯 Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personal Knowledge Management (PKM)">
    **Obsidian** and **Notion** are the top choices for building a second brain. Obsidian excels with its local storage and linking; Notion offers databases and team collaboration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Project Management">
    **Notion** and **Linear** handle team coordination. Notion is preferred for content/ops teams; Linear dominates engineering workflows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Daily Task Management">
    **Todoist** remains the gold standard for personal GTD-style task management, with a natural language parser that makes adding tasks frictionless.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document Writing & Collaboration">
    **Notion** and **Craft** both offer beautiful document writing experiences, with Notion winning on collaboration and Craft winning on design and Apple integration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
