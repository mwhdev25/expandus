Marketing has been transformed by AI. What once took a team of 10 can now be accomplished by a sharp marketer with the right tools. This collection covers the best AI tools across every area of the modern marketing stack — from content creation to campaign analytics.

---

## The List

### 1. ChatGPT — AI Copywriting & Ideation

**[ChatGPT →](/tools/chatgpt)** `AI & LLMs` `Freemium`

The starting point for most AI-powered marketing workflows. Use ChatGPT to draft ad copy, write email sequences, brainstorm campaign angles, and repurpose content across formats.

**Best for:** First drafts, ideation, content repurposing

---

### 2. Claude — In-Depth Writing & Long-Form Content

**[Claude →](/tools/claude)** `AI & LLMs` `Freemium`

When quality and nuance matter more than speed, Claude is the better choice. Excellent for long-form blog posts, brand voice matching, and editing large documents.

**Best for:** Long-form content, editing, brand tone

---

### 3. Midjourney — AI Image Generation

**Midjourney** `Design & Creative` `Paid`

Generate photorealistic campaign visuals, product mockups, and lifestyle imagery without a photographer or illustration team. The highest-quality text-to-image AI available.

**Best for:** Social visuals, ad creatives, brand imagery

---

### 4. Figma — Design & Creative Production

**[Figma →](/tools/figma)** `Design & Creative` `Freemium`

Create on-brand social graphics, ad templates, landing page mockups, and email headers with the same tool your design team uses. Canva is the simpler alternative.

**Best for:** Branded templates, design collaboration

---

### 5. HubSpot — CRM & Marketing Automation

**HubSpot** `Marketing & Sales` `Freemium`

The most complete marketing platform — manage contacts, email campaigns, landing pages, social scheduling, and reporting in one place with built-in AI features.

**Best for:** Full-funnel marketing, email nurturing, CRM

---

### 6. Semrush — SEO & Competitive Research

**Semrush** `Marketing & Sales` `Paid`

Identify the keywords your audience searches for, audit your site's SEO health, and spy on competitor strategies. An indispensable tool for organic growth.

**Best for:** SEO, keyword research, competitor analysis

---

### 7. Beehiiv — Newsletter & Creator Marketing

**Beehiiv** `Marketing & Sales` `Freemium`

The best newsletter platform for growth-focused creators and brands. Built-in referral programs, monetization, and subscriber analytics make it the modern Substack alternative.

**Best for:** Email newsletters, creator monetization

---

### 8. Copy.ai — Marketing Copy at Scale

**Copy.ai** `Marketing & Sales` `Freemium`

Purpose-built AI for marketing copy — product descriptions, Google Ads, LinkedIn posts, landing page headlines. Great for scaling content output across many formats.

**Best for:** Ad copy, social captions, product descriptions

---

### 9. Zapier — Marketing Automation

**[Zapier →](/tools/zapier)** `Automation` `Freemium`

Connect your entire marketing stack — automatically sync leads from Facebook Ads to HubSpot, trigger welcome email sequences, and generate weekly reports without manual work.

**Best for:** Connecting apps, automating lead workflows

---

### 10. Notion — Content Calendar & Marketing Wiki

**[Notion →](/tools/notion)** `Productivity` `Freemium`

The power marketing hub — run your editorial calendar, manage campaign briefs, store brand assets, and document your playbooks, all in one organized workspace.

**Best for:** Editorial calendars, campaign management, wikis

---

## 🎯 Suggested Stack by Team Size

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Solo Marketer / Freelancer">
    Start with **ChatGPT** (writing), **Canva** (design), and **Beehiiv** (email). Add **Zapier** when you're ready to automate. Under $30/mo total.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Early-Stage Startup (1–5 person team)">
    **Claude** + **ChatGPT**, **Figma**, **HubSpot** (free CRM), **Semrush** (one seat). Automate with **Zapier** or **Make**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Growth-Stage Team (10+ people)">
    Full HubSpot suite, dedicated Midjourney seats for creative, Semrush for SEO, Zapier Team plan for automation. Add **Copy.ai** for high-volume content production.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
