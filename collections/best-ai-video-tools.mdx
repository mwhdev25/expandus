AI has unlocked a new era for video production. What once required a studio, camera crew, and editing suite can now be accomplished with a browser tab. This collection covers the best AI video tools for marketers, creators, and teams.

---

## The List

### 1. Runway — AI Video Generation & Editing

**Runway** `Design & Creative` `Freemium`

The leading AI video platform. Generate videos from text or images, apply AI effects, remove backgrounds, and edit with natural language prompts. Gen-3 Alpha produces stunning results.

**Best for:** Creative professionals, agencies, AI video art
**Starting Price:** Free (limited) / $12/mo

---

### 2. OpenAI Sora — Text-to-Video at Scale

**Sora** `AI & LLMs` `Paid`

OpenAI's text-to-video model. Generate high-quality, realistic video scenes from written prompts. Still early access, but producing industry-leading results.

**Best for:** High-quality text-to-video generation
**Starting Price:** Included in ChatGPT Pro ($200/mo)

---

### 3. HeyGen — AI Avatars & Video Personalization

**HeyGen** `Design & Creative` `Freemium`

Create talking-head videos with AI avatars — no camera, no studio. Ideal for personalized outreach videos, product demos, and training content at scale.

**Best for:** Marketing personalization, sales outreach, L&D
**Starting Price:** Free (1 min/mo) / $24/mo

---

### 4. Descript — AI-Powered Video & Podcast Editing

**Descript** `Design & Creative` `Freemium`

Edit video by editing the transcript. Remove filler words, silences, and retake scenes — all with AI. The best all-in-one tool for podcast and video creators.

**Best for:** Podcasters, content creators, screen recordings
**Starting Price:** Free / $12/mo

---

### 5. Loom — Async Video Messaging

**Loom** `Productivity` `Freemium`

Record and share quick video messages for async communication. AI-generates transcripts, summaries, and action items from every recording.

**Best for:** Remote teams, sales videos, internal comms
**Starting Price:** Free (25 videos) / $12.50/user/mo

---

### 6. Captions — AI Short-Form Video

**Captions** `Design & Creative` `Freemium`

AI-powered short-form video creator for social media. Auto-captions, teleprompter, AI B-roll, and style presets. Designed for building a creator presence on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

**Best for:** Social media creators, personal branding
**Starting Price:** Free / $7.99/mo
