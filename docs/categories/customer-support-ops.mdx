## 🎯 Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Live Chat & Messaging">
    **Intercom** and **Crisp** both offer real-time chat with customers. Intercom has deeper AI automation; Crisp is more affordable for smaller teams.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ticketing & Issue Tracking">
    **Zendesk** and **Freshdesk** are the mature players for high-volume ticketing. Plain is the modern alternative for product-led SaaS companies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Internal Tools & Admin Panels">
    **Retool** allows operations teams to build powerful internal dashboards and tools on top of existing databases without a dedicated engineering resource.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Support Automation">
    **Intercom's Fin AI** can resolve up to 50% of support queries automatically. Combined with a knowledge base, this significantly reduces support volume.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
