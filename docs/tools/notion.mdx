## About Notion

Notion is a flexible, block-based workspace that combines notes, documents, databases, kanban boards, calendars, and wikis in a single product. It's the backbone of many startups' internal operations — from roadmaps and wikis to hiring pipelines and content calendars.

**Website:** [notion.so](https://notion.so)

## 🎯 Use Cases

- **Team Wikis:** Centralized company knowledge base and documentation
- **Project Management:** Roadmaps, sprint planning, content calendars
- **Personal PKM:** Second brain, meeting notes, reading lists
- **CRM:** Simple contact/deal tracking with database views
- **Hiring:** Applicant tracking, interview notes, offer letters
- **Content Pipeline:** Editorial workflows from brief to published

---

## ✅ Pros

- Incredible flexibility — one tool for many use cases
- Beautiful, minimal UI that doesn't feel like enterprise software
- Strong free tier for individuals
- Powerful relational databases with multiple views
- Thriving template and community ecosystem

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Can get slow with very large databases
- Offline mode is limited compared to local-first tools like Obsidian
- Learning curve for database/relational features
- Notion AI is an add-on cost ($8/user/mo extra)
