## About Zapier

Zapier is the world's leading no-code automation platform. It lets you connect any two (or more) apps with "Zaps" — automated workflows triggered by events. No development skills required. With over 6,000 app integrations, Zapier is the fastest way to eliminate repetitive tasks and connect your entire software stack.

**Website:** [zapier.com](https://zapier.com)

## 🎯 Use Cases

- **Lead Management:** Auto-add form submissions to CRM, send welcome emails
- **Notifications:** Ping Slack when a new sale closes in Stripe or Shopify
- **Content Publishing:** Publish new blog posts → social media automatically
- **Data Sync:** Sync contacts between HubSpot and Mailchimp
- **Reporting:** Weekly digest emails from Google Sheets data
- **Customer Onboarding:** Trigger sequences when a new user signs up

---

## ✅ Pros

- Easiest no-code automation tool for beginners
- Largest app integration library in any automation platform
- Robust error handling and monitoring dashboard
- AI integration for intelligent automation steps
- Pre-built templates dramatically reduce setup time

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Pricing scales steeply with task volume (heavy users pay a lot)
- Slower execution than Make or n8n for complex workflows
- Limited data transformation vs Make's scenario builder
- Multi-step Zaps require paid plan
- For developers, n8n or Pipedream offer more flexibility
