## About Claude

Claude is the flagship AI assistant from Anthropic — built with a focus on safety, nuance, and helpfulness. Claude's hallmark is its long context window (up to 200K tokens), making it the best AI for working with long documents, detailed analysis, and extended conversations.

**Website:** [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)

## 🎯 Use Cases

- **Long-Document Analysis:** Summarizing research papers, contracts, annual reports
- **Writing & Editing:** First drafts, editing for tone, academic writing
- **Legal & Compliance:** Document review, precedent research, drafting
- **Customer Support Scripts:** Empathetic, on-brand response drafting
- **Coding:** Code review, documentation, and debugging with context
- **Research Synthesis:** Pulling insights from multiple uploaded sources

---

## ✅ Pros

- Best-in-class long-context performance (200K tokens)
- Nuanced, human-like writing output
- Thoughtful, well-reasoned responses with fewer hallucinations
- Excellent document and PDF handling
- Strong adherence to instructions and formatting requirements

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- No real-time web browsing (as of early 2026)
- No image generation (unlike ChatGPT + DALL·E)
- Smaller third-party ecosystem vs ChatGPT
- Can be overly cautious on sensitive topics
